T10 DECODER

SUMMARY

  • For Märklin and TRIX C-Track turnouts.
  • DCC and MM compatible.
  • Fast and simple one-button programming.

CONNECTOR

ADD TO CART

DESCRIPTION

The T10 decoder is suitable for Märklin and TRIX C-Track turnouts. The decoder is capable of operating with both DCC and MM with 2044 and 320 addresses respectively. The protocol used to program addresses is remembered and adhered to in operation. The decoder has a light output for connection of an LED turnout lantern. The two motor outputs and lantern output can each be given unique addresses. The lantern can be controlled by a separate address, static, or switch with the turnout position. The lantern switches on and off with a fading effect. The decoder remembers the turnout position and lantern state when power is lost. The decoder is programmed by a programming button accessable underneath the turnout once installed. Two programming modes give maximum flexibility of address setting without complex CV programming.
